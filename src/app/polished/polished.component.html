"An on-demand application bringing nail-artists to you."


Sign-up for a customer account. Or if you're a license nail technician, sign-up for a vender account.

Once logged in, you can use our booking page to search for a nail artist around your area.

If you want more options you can also search for more nail artists around you.

You can see user generated pictures and reviews of any nail artist.

When you find someone you like, book the artist and pay for their service with a credit card or paypal.

Customers have the ability to rate their experience, upload a picture of their nails, and even leave a comment.

Venders have the ability to keep track of their upcoming appointments and get monthly income analytics.

Venders also have the ability to get directions to and from their customers.

Sign-up for a customer account or if you're a license nail technician, sign-up for a vender account.

Once logged in, you can use our booking page to search for a nail artist around your area.

If you want more options, search more artist and see whose available around you.

When you find someone you like, click on on their profile to see user generated pictures and reviews of the artist.

Once you find someone you like, book the artist and pay for their service using a credit card or paypal.

After the service has been provided, you can review the nail artist. Users have the ability to rate their experience, upload a picture of the result service, and/or leave a comment.

As a vender, you have the ability to keep track of your upcoming appointments and get analytics regarding your monthly income and customer rating.

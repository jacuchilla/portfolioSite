Point Blank is a social platform for learning about publics perception of the most influential individuals today.

Login/Signup

To begin learning about todays most influential people, login to your account or signup using facebook. Our authentication is provided through Passport.

Serch for Influential People

See Their Profile

When you find someone you like, click on on their profile to see more information. Users can see pictures of that artist previous work, as well as customer reviews.

Book Nail Artist

Once the user finds someone they like, they can book the artist and pay for their service using a credit card or paypal.

Review Nail Artist

After the service has been provided, users can review the nail artist. Users have the ability to rate their experience, upload a picture of the result service, and/or leave a comment.

Nail Artist Dashboard

As a vender, nail artists have the ability keep track of their upcoming appointments and get analytics regarding their monthly income/rating.

Point Blank

"Crowdsourcing public opinion to better understand people."


Point Blank is a social platform that uncovers how the public feels about today's most influential individuals.

Our name says it all. Our platform provides a space for people to express their opinions.

To being, login to your account or signup using facebook. Our authentication is provided by Passport.

Once logged in, search through your person of interest and get a quick feel for their overall general rating.

When you find someone you're interested in, click on their profile to see how the general population feels about that person.

Teach Me How

"A social learning platform that connects teachers with students."


Teach Me How gives people a platform to teach and learn.

Securely login or signup via Facebook, Gmail, or LinkedIn.

Modify your account and see a list of your current classes.

If you're interested in learning, search for a class or browse through classes.

Classes can be taught in person or online via our live videostreaming feature.

Live 1:1 meetings are also available through our tutoring service.

If you're interested in teaching, decide what you want to teach and create a class.

Create a request ticket for a subject not being offered.

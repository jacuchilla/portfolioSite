Teach Me How

"A social learning platform that connects teachers with students."




Teach Me How connects people interested in teaching with people interested in learning.

Securely login or signup via Facebook, Gmail, or LinkedIn.

Modify your account and see a list of you're current classes.

If you're interested in learning, browse through the classes being taught or specifically search for a class.

Once you choose a class navigate to the classroom portal and prepare to learn via our live videostreaming feature. Use the chat functionality to post questions during class.

You can also attend 1:1 meetings by clicking on the tutoring button. When your teacher is ready, log into the video chat and prepare to have a 1:1 with your instructor.

If you're interested in teaching a class, create a class. As an instructor, you have the option of teaching in person or online by adding your Youtube channel ID.

If a class you're interested in is not being offered, create a request ticket for someone to teach the class.
